What is True Money Supply?

The True Money Supply (TMS) is a measure of the money stock developed by Murray Rothbard and Joseph Salerno. Unlike M1 and M2, TMS exclusively tracks money available for exchange without delay or penalty. This distinction has significance, as only immediately available money affects prices and economic calculation.

By tracking TMS, we gain clearer insight into monetary inflation than with price indices like the CPI, which are influenced by non-monetary factors.

TMS data isn't widely accessible. This website aggregates the necessary components from the FRED API, providing a current and convenient way to track TMS.
