What is True Money Supply?

The True Money Supply (TMS) is a measure of the money stock developed by Murray Rothbard and Joseph Salerno. Unlike conventional measures such as M1 or M2, TMS focuses exclusively on money available for exchange without delay or penalty. This distinction has significance, as only immediately available money affects prices and economic calculation.

By tracking TMS, we gain clearer insight into monetary inflation than with price indices like the CPI, which are influenced by non-monetary factors.
